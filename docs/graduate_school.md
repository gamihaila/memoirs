I enrolled in graduate school in September 1994. Ioana was entering her second
year at the time. We moved again, this time into a one-bedroom apartment in one
of the Graduate Family Housing buildings on Charles Street, at the North-East
edge of campus. After the long commute to work, this was heaven: we were ten
minutes away from the CS department, walking through Queen's Park, often
stopping on the way for a workout in the Hart House. The campus architecture was
gorgeous, with a Collegiate Gothic style mixed with Romanesque Revival.
